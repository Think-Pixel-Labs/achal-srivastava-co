Common questions

Helpful answers before you call.

Do you handle both individual and business tax filings?

Yes. The firm supports ITR planning and filing for individuals as well as GST, TDS, audit and compliance work for businesses.

Can you help register a new company or startup?

Yes. Services include Private Limited, LLP, OPC, Startup India, NGO, Trust, Society and Section 8 company registration support.

What documents are needed for a first consultation?

Bring or share your PAN, Aadhaar, bank details, income records, previous returns, GST details if any, and the specific notice or compliance requirement you want reviewed.
